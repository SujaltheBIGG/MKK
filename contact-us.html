FAQ

What is your return policy

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy on all unworn items in their original packaging. Simply contact our customer care team and we'll provide a prepaid return label. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return. For hygiene reasons, earrings that have been worn cannot be returned, but we're happy to assist with exchanges if there's a sizing issue.

How can I clean my jewellery

Gently polish your MKK jewellery with the soft cloth included with every order. For a deeper clean, use warm water with a drop of mild soap, then pat dry with a lint-free cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals, perfumes, and chlorinated water. Store each piece in its original pouch to prevent scratching and tarnishing, and your jewellery will continue to sparkle for years to come.

How long does shipping take

Standard shipping within the US takes 3-5 business days and is complimentary on all orders over $50. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for a flat fee of $9.95. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days depending on your location. Every order includes tracking, and we'll send you updates at each stage so you always know when to expect your delivery.
